Map War Guide

War Intel

This panel explains the current map-war labels without exposing hidden formulas or backend-only values.

War Contribution

Your personal weekly war activity from Patrol, Donate, and some Siege actions.

Faction Control

Who is currently winning or holding a node on the map.

Pressure

A conflict signal only. It shows where fighting is rising, but it does not directly decide capture.

Siege Participation

Siege-specific actions and results at active siege nodes.

Blood-Moon Damage

Damage dealt inside the Blood-Moon event only.

Archive Progress

Burned Archive progress for your faction. It is progress, not points.

Contract Contribution

Progress inside Broken Contracts only.

War Rewards

Rewards from war-adjacent systems. They are not one universal currency.

What Your Actions Usually Affect

  • Patrol and Donate can affect Faction Control and War Contribution.
  • Siege affects Siege Participation and can affect control outcomes.
  • Blood-Moon attacks affect Blood-Moon Damage and event progress.
  • Burned Archive attempts affect Archive Progress.
